# **App Name**: Digital Authority Hub

## Core Features:

- Identity Verification System: A dynamic search input system allowing users to verify IDs against a Firebase Firestore database, displaying 'Verified' status for official validation.
- Official Notice Board: Displays all official announcements with titles, reference numbers, and dates. New notices feature a blinking 'New' indicator and an integrated QR code for authenticity.
- Dynamic Achievement Stats: Visually appealing statistical counters that animate upon scroll, showcasing key achievements and impact, such as 'Verified Members' and 'System Security'.
- Administrative Content Management: An administrative interface for BDSA staff to efficiently manage and update all dynamic content, including notices, achievement statistics, and frequently asked questions.
- Official Web Structure & Navigation: A comprehensive website structure with an official top bar, header navigation (Home, About, Services, Notice, Team, FAQ, Contact), and a prominent 'Verify Certificate/ID' button, designed for easy navigation.
- AI-powered Announcement Summarizer Tool: An internal AI tool for administrators to automatically generate concise summaries of new official announcements or news insights, streamlining content creation and dissemination.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: A premium dark theme to evoke authority and seriousness. Background: Deep charcoal with a subtle warm undertone (#1C1915).
- Primary color: Glowing golden yellow (#E6AC2E) to highlight important information and interactive elements, representing premium quality and official status.
- Accent color: A rich burnt orange (#993D26) for secondary emphasis and complementary contrast, maintaining an elegant, warm palette.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel, befitting an authoritative entity.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for objective readability, ensuring clarity and modernity in official communications.
- Golden-themed official seals and icons for legal validity and service representation. All other interactive icons will be clean, minimalist, and integrated within the dark and gold palette.
- A clean, modular layout with strong visual hierarchy and generous white space. Content sections are clearly defined against the dark background, facilitating an easy, professional user experience.
- Subtle, smooth animations such as the hero section's subtle technology backdrop, scroll-triggered achievement counters, blinking 'New' indicators for notices, and gentle transitions for interactive elements.
